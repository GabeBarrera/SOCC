SOC COMMANDER

Four hidden attack stages are loose somewhere on the company's global network. You have ten turns, ten procedures and a d20. Find the kill chain before the attackers finish the job.
New Game · Office
The short version
The Incident Captain secretly holds 4 attack cards — one per stage of the kill chain — each planted at a company site on the globe. Each turn, the Defenders discuss, play one Procedure, and roll a d20.
  • 11+ succeeds. Established procedures roll at +3; improvised ones at +0.
  • If the procedure can detect a hidden card, it flips — and its site lights up on the globe.
  • Played procedures cool down for 3 turns. Some injects can remove a procedure entirely.
  • A natural 1, natural 20, or 3 fails in a row draws one of ten Injects. Injects are… events.
  • Reveal all 4 cards within 10 turns to win. Drag the globe to look around — it shows where the attack has reached, nothing more.
